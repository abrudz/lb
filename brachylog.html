Brachylog language bar and character compositions

This provides two ways to easily enter Brachylog characters into pretty much any input field on any webpage:

  1. Enter a combo sequence and hit tab, e.g. (=Tab gives ⊆.
  2. Click on symbols in the language bar.

Desktop browser

  1. Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Brachylog

Webpage

Activation

  1. Navigate to the web page where you want to enter Brachylog characters.
  2. Click on the Brachylog bookmark. The language bar should appear at the web page's top.
